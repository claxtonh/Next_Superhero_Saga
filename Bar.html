Super Heros Breakout by Gender

It was interesting to see the breakout of gender in the comic book super heroes. We expected to see a majority of male characters and that was true but 1 out of 3 superheros being female was not expected. If we would continue this project looking at the power level of charecters in addition to gender would be interesting to see if the extremes also skew towards men or if we see a difference in the extremes.
