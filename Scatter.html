The bigger they are, the harder they fall

Turns out, this trope isn't always the case. Most superheroes cluster around being average height or weight, with more variety found on the weight side. However, we see superheroes at the extremes, either super tall or super heavy. But we don't have many superheroes that fall in both categories.
